User Guide

General Tool Hints

  • Motion blur shows fast movement.
  • The most important poses - keyframes are drawn with darker colors.

Orientation

  • Direction the skeleton is facing is indicated with pink line drawn from the head.
  • Right-sided limbs are color-coded with blue. Left-sided limbs are color-coded with red.

Minimap

  • Movement on minimap always starts from the middle of the map.
  • The colors on the minimap go from red/yellowish (beginning of sequence) to purple/blueish (end of sequence). Black squares signify keyframes.
  • Each square of the grid on the minimap is roughly 1*1m large.

Time

  • The length of the sequence in seconds is shown in the bottom right of the map.
  • Sequences are scaled by their length.
